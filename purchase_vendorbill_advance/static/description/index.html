Purchase Order Down Payments


Description

Allows a user to register down payments against purchase orders and apply down payments to the final bill from the vendor.

Configuration

Settings -> Purchase -> Down Payment

  • Create a Down Payment Product
  • Set Expense account on product in the accounting tab
  • On the Purchase tab, set the product to "Ordered Quantities"
  • Product Type = Service

    • Functionality

    After validating a purchase order, use the Create Bill -> Down payment (percentage) or Down payment (fixed amount) to generate a bill. Multiple Down payments can be made on one purchase order.

    When creating the final bill, use the Invoiceable lines (deduct down payments) option to apply the down payment to the final bill.
