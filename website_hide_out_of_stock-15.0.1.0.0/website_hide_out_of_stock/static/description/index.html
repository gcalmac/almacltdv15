Website Auto Hide Out Of Stock-Products

Display only in-stock products using Odoo Website Auto Hide Out Of Stock Products!

Odoo Website Auto Hide Out Of Stock Products enables you to hide all out-of-stock products. Moreover, all the products contain an in-stock tag on them. The cron or dynamic product type lets you update product stock conveniently. Additionally, you can define the text and font color from the backend and manage the hidden products.
